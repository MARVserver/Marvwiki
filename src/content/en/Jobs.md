# Jobs Management Guide

On the MARV server, you can join professions to earn money and experience through daily activities.

## 1. Joining a Job
Browse the available professions and pick one that suits your playstyle.

- **Browse Jobs**: `/jobs browse`
  - Opens a GUI showing all available jobs and their rewards.
- **Join a Job**: `/jobs join {jobname}`
  - Example: `/jobs join miner`
  - There is a limit to how many jobs you can join at once.

## 2. Earning Rewards
Perform tasks related to your job to automatically receive money and XP.

- **Check Stats**: `/jobs stats`
  - View your current level and experience progress.
- **Leaderboards**: `/jobs top {jobname}`
  - See the top workers in a specific profession.

## 3. Spending Job Points
As you work, you earn "Job Points" in addition to money.

- **Job Shop**: `/jobs shop`
  - Spend points on special items and perks.

## 4. Daily Quests
Complete unique tasks every day for extra rewards.

- **Check Quests**: `/jobs quests`
