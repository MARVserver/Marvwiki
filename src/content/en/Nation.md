# Nation Management Guide

Form a powerful collection of towns known as a Nation.

## 1. Founding a Nation
Establish a nation with your town as the capital.

- **Command**: `/nation create {name}`
  - Requires a significant founding fee.

## 2. Inviting Towns
Grow your influence by inviting other towns.

- **Invite**: `/nation invite {town_name}`

## 3. Diplomacy
Set your relationship with other nations.

- **Ally**: `/nation ally {nation_name}`
- **Enemy**: `/nation enemy {nation_name}`

## 4. Taxes and Upkeep
Nations also require daily upkeep.

- **Deposit**: `/nation deposit {amount}`
- **Set Taxes**: `/nation set taxes {amount}`
  - Collect taxes from member towns.
