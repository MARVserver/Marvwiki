# QuickShop-Hikari User Guide

On the MARV server, you can open your own shop using chest-based shops.

## 1. How to Create a Shop
1. Place a **Chest**.
2. Hold the **Item** you want to sell in your hand.
3. Left-click the chest.
4. Type the **Price per item** in the chat and press Enter.
5. A sign and a floating item display will appear, and your shop is ready!

## 2. Managing Your Shop
Interact with the sign (Left-click to buy or Right-click for menu) or use the following commands.

### Main Commands
- `/qs create {price}`: Create a shop with the item in your hand.
- `/qs price {price}`: Change the price of the items.
- `/qs buy`: Switch the shop to "Buying Mode" (buy items from players).
- `/qs sell`: Switch the shop to "Selling Mode" (default).
- `/qs remove`: Remove the shop.

### Features
- **Control Panel**: Right-clicking the sign opens a GUI for easy management.
- **Staff**: Use `/qs staff add {player}` to allow friends to manage your stock.

## 3. Finding Items
- `/qs find {item_name}`: Find the nearest shop selling a specific item.
